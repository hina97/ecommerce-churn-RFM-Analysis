<h1>📊 E-Commerce Customer Churn & RFM Segmentation Analysis</h1>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>
<p>
This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior in an e-commerce platform to understand engagement patterns, purchasing trends, and customer retention. The analysis applies <b>Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)</b> and <b>RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) segmentation</b> to identify valuable customer groups and detect potential churn patterns.
</p>

<p>
By examining user activity, transaction behavior, and customer value metrics, the project aims to generate actionable insights that can help businesses improve customer retention and enhance marketing strategies.
</p>

<h2>📂 Dataset Description</h2>
<p>
The dataset contains customer demographic information, engagement metrics, and purchasing behavior from an e-commerce platform.
</p>

<p><b>Key features in the dataset include:</b></p>

<ul>
<li><b>Customer Demographics</b> – Age, gender, country, and city information</li>
<li><b>Engagement Metrics</b> – Login frequency, pages per session, and browsing activity</li>
<li><b>Purchase Behavior</b> – Total purchases, average order value, and lifetime value</li>
<li><b>Discount Usage</b> – Customer usage patterns of discounts and promotions</li>
<li><b>Customer Support Interaction</b> – Customer service calls and return rate</li>
<li><b>Churn Indicator</b> – Whether a customer is active or has churned</li>
</ul>

<p>
These variables provide a comprehensive view of customer activity and help analyze factors influencing customer retention and purchasing behavior.
</p>

<h2>🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)</h2>
<p>
Exploratory Data Analysis was performed to understand the structure of the dataset and uncover patterns in customer behavior. The analysis included examining distributions, relationships between variables, and identifying trends across different customer groups.
</p>

<p>The EDA process helped identify:</p>

<ul>
<li>Customer engagement patterns</li>
<li>Purchasing trends and spending behavior</li>
<li>Relationships between behavioral metrics and revenue indicators</li>
<li>Potential indicators of customer churn</li>
</ul>

<p>
Through systematic exploration of the dataset, meaningful insights were derived to support further analysis and segmentation.
</p>

<h2>📊 Data Visualization</h2>
<p>
Data visualization plays an important role in understanding patterns within the dataset. Various visualizations were created to represent customer behavior, engagement levels, and purchasing activity in a clear and interpretable way.
</p>

<p>
The visual analysis helps highlight relationships between different variables and reveals important trends in customer interactions with the platform. By transforming raw data into graphical representations, the visualizations make it easier to interpret customer activity, identify behavioral patterns, and explore differences across customer segments.
</p>

<p>
Overall, the visualizations provide valuable insights that support data-driven decision making and improve the understanding of customer engagement and retention.
</p>

<h2>🧩 Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)</h2>
<p>
RFM analysis was applied to segment customers based on three key behavioral metrics:
</p>

<ul>
<li><b>Recency</b> – How recently a customer made a purchase</li>
<li><b>Frequency</b> – How often the customer makes purchases</li>
<li><b>Monetary Value</b> – How much the customer spends</li>
</ul>

<p>
Based on these metrics, customers were grouped into different segments such as <b>Champions, Loyal Customers, Frequent Buyers, At Risk Customers, and Others</b>.
</p>

<p>
This segmentation helps businesses identify their most valuable customers and develop targeted strategies for improving engagement and retention.
</p>

<h2>📈 Key Insights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Customer engagement metrics such as login frequency and browsing activity show strong relationships with purchasing behavior.</li>
<li>High-value customers tend to demonstrate consistent purchasing patterns and frequent platform interaction.</li>
<li>Certain customer groups show reduced activity levels, indicating potential churn risk.</li>
<li>Behavioral metrics provide stronger insights into customer value compared to demographic factors.</li>
</ul>

<h2>💡 Recommendations</h2>

<ul>
<li>Develop targeted marketing campaigns for high-value customer segments.</li>
<li>Implement retention strategies for customers showing declining engagement.</li>
<li>Provide personalized promotions and discounts to encourage repeat purchases.</li>
<li>Improve customer engagement through loyalty programs and targeted communication.</li>
<li>Monitor behavioral indicators regularly to detect early signs of churn.</li>
</ul>
